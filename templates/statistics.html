Statistics

On the right, you can see how many images have been checked so far, and how many were checked during the last 24 hours. The pie chart shows the fraction of checked images flagged as OK vs. those flagged with at least one Problem.

Below you find a break-up of the images that have been found problematic. Red areas denote the percentage of those image with a given problem, while Blue areas indicate the corresponding false-positive fraction (see our Tutorial if you have questions about the false positives). When you click on any of the pie charts, you can see examples of images that have been found with this kind of problem.

There can be multiple problems in any given image, therefore the proportions do not necessarily add up to one.

Also check out our Heat Map for the spatial locations of problems aggregated over detectors. If you want to access the individual problem reports directly, use our API.
